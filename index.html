How it all started

Metin's parents used to live in Turkey since 1980, that year everything had changed. They came to Denmark and in 1990 opened a restaurant in Viby Square. Metin got a leisure job in the restaurant and his 2 brothers helped when needed. In the beginning, the restaurant had just 10 square meters and now there are seats for 90 people.
